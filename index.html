    About the Project

    Fuel Reach is a mobile application designed to deliver fuel to users anytime and anywhere. It helps people in emergency situations when they run out of fuel.

    Problem Statement

    Many people face issues when their vehicle runs out of fuel in remote areas or during emergencies. Finding a nearby fuel station is not always possible, especially during night hours or in unfamiliar locations.

    Our Solution

    The Fuel Reach app allows users to order fuel directly to their location using their smartphone. It ensures quick and safe fuel delivery with real-time tracking and professional drivers.

    Key Features

    • Live location tracking
    • Fuel booking (Petrol/Diesel)
    • Online payment options
    • Real-time notifications
    • Order tracking

    How It Works

    1. User opens the app and logs in
    2. Selects fuel type and quantity (Petrol/Diesel)
    3. Shares current location
    4. Makes payment through preferred method
    5. Delivery agent delivers fuel to your location

    Technology Used

    • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
    • Mapping: Leaflet.js & OpenStreetMap
    • Storage: Local Storage (Client-side)
    • APIs: Geolocation API
